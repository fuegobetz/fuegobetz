Sharp Wallet Tracking

The core signal engine

We track hundreds of on-chain wallets with verified long-term positive ROI across thousands of markets. When these wallets move into a position, we detect it in real time and score the conviction based on trade size, recency, and historical edge. Only wallets with ≥3 markets traded and a sharpness score above the qualification threshold are surfaced.

📐

Kelly Criterion Sizing

Quarter Kelly, 3% hard cap

Every alert includes a Kelly-optimized bet size. Full Kelly formula: f* = (b·p − q) / b, where b = net odds and p = sharp-implied true probability. We apply Quarter Kelly (0.25×) for variance reduction, then hard-cap at 3% of your bankroll regardless of how large the raw Kelly fraction is. On an $10,000 bankroll, the max bet is $300.
